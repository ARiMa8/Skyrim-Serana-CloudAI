Serana, follow me, wait here, let's trade, attack that nearest enemy and eat this sweetroll. Our journey spans holds, from the markets of Whiterun to the graveyards of Falkreath. We’ll navigate the piers of Dawnstar, dodge intrigue in Morthal, and climb the stone terraces of Markarth. Whether enduring frozen winds in Windhelm or sailing to the ashen wastes of Solstheim, we must cross Dragon Bridge to avoid Thalmor patrols. While mages study at the College of Winterhold, pilgrims rest in Ivarstead before ascending High Hrothgar. Deep in the mountains, the Dwemer ruins of Arkngthamz hide mechanical centurions, far from dragons terrorizing Kynesgrove. As the Dragonborn, we face Draugr Deathlords and Falmer Shadowmasters. From the shadows of Castle Volkihar to the Dawnguard fort, we wield Auriel’s Bow and the Wabbajack to vanquish Chaurus Reapers and secure the glowing artifacts of Blackreach. Our legend is etched into the tundra of Skyrim.
